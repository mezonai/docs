# Overview Settings

<Image style={{ margin: '1rem 2rem' }}  src={overviewSettingsImage001} alt="Overview Settings 001" />

Open **Clan Settings**, and you will see the following sections:

* [**Overview**](./overview-settings/overview): Set your Clan’s name, change its avatar, and update the banner.
* [**Roles & Permissions Management**](./overview-settings/roles-and-permissions-management): Create, organize, and assign permissions for each role within the Clan.
* [**Icons & Stickers (Image/Voice)**](./overview-settings/emoji-image-and-voice-stickers): Upload and manage the Clan’s custom emojis, image stickers, and voice stickers.
* [**Category Order**](./overview-settings/category-order): Arrange the display order of your Clan’s categories and channels.
* [**Integrations**](./overview-settings/integrations): Connect with bots or external apps to extend features and personalize your Clan.
* [**Audit Logs**](./overview-settings/audit-log): View the history of administrative actions within the Clan.
* [**New Member Guide**](./overview-settings/new-member-guide): Set up guides and default roles for members joining your Clan.
* [**Community Activation**](./overview-settings/enable-community): Enable community features, allowing your Clan to have a stable link and open access for anyone to join freely.
* [**Delete Clan**](./overview-settings/delete-clan): Remove the Clan from the system.

Wish you a stronger and more professional community with Mezon!
