### How to Manage Members

1. In the left navigation bar, select **Members**.

<img src={memberManagement001} alt="member-management-001" />

2. Here, you can view the full list of members in your Clan.\
   Use the **search** function to quickly find a specific person.

<img src={memberManagement002} alt="member-management-002" />

3. When you right-click on a member’s name, you can:

* View the member’s profile.
* Send a private message to the member.
* Transfer Clan ownership.
* Remove the member from the Clan if necessary.

<img src={memberManagement003} alt="member-management-003" />

4. You can quickly assign roles to each member by clicking the “+” sign in the **Role** column:

<img src={memberManagement004} alt="member-management-004" />
</TabItem>
<TabItem value="mobile" label="Mobile">
In **Clan Settings**, you can access **Member Management**.

<img src={memberManagementMobile001} alt="member-management-mobile-001" />

Here, you can view the full list of members in your Clan.\
Use the search bar to quickly find a specific person.

When you **click** on a **member’s name**, you can:

* Edit their roles
* Transfer Clan ownership
* Remove the member from the Clan if necessary
</TabItem>
