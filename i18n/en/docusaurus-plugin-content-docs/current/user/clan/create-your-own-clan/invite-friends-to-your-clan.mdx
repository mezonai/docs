# Invite Friends to Your Clan
<Tabs>
<TabItem value="PC" label="PC">
1. Click on your **Clan name**.
2. Select **Invite People** from the menu.

<Image style={{ margin: '1rem 2rem' }} src={inviteFriendsToYourClan001}></Image>

3. You can invite friends in two ways:

* **Direct Invite:** Choose friends from your list and click **Invite**.
* **Copy Invite Link:** Share the invite link via chat, email, or any other platform.

<Image style={{ margin: '1rem 2rem' }} src={inviteFriendsToYourClan002}></Image>

:::tip
You can also copy the **invite link with a QR code**, making it easy to share with multiple people and grow your Clan quickly.
:::
</TabItem>
<TabItem value="mobile" label="Mobile">
### **How to Invite Friends:**

1. Click on the **Clan name** you want to invite others to.
2. Select **“Invites.”**

<div align="left"><img style={{ width: '30%' }} src={inviteFriendsToYourClanMobile001} alt="invite-friends-to-your-clan-mobile-001" /></div>

3. Choose one of the three invite methods:

<div align="left"><img style={{ width: '30%' }} src={inviteFriendsToYourClanMobile002} alt="invite-friends-to-your-clan-mobile-002" /></div>

* **Direct Invite:** Select friends from your list and click **Invite.**
* **Share Link:** Copy and send the invite link via message, email, or any platform.
* **QR Code:** Share the Clan’s invite QR image for others to scan and join instantly.
</TabItem>
