# Transferring Clan Ownership
When you need to change the administrator or hand over the community, you can transfer Clan ownership to ensure continuous management and smooth operation.

<Tabs>
<TabItem value="PC" label="PC">
### **Steps to Transfer Ownership:**

1. In the left menu, select **Members**.

![transferring-clan-ownership-001](./images/transferring-clan-ownership-001.png)

2. Right-click on the member you want to transfer ownership to, then select **Transfer Ownership**.

![transferring-clan-ownership-002](./images/transferring-clan-ownership-002.png)

3. Check the confirmation box, then click **Transfer Ownership** to complete the process.

![transferring-clan-ownership-003](./images/transferring-clan-ownership-003.png)
</TabItem>
<TabItem value="mobile" label="Mobile">
### How to Transfer Clan Ownership

You can assign full Clan management to another member.

**Method 1: Through Clan Settings**

1. Click your **Clan name**, then select the **Settings icon**![transferring-clan-ownership-mobile-001](./images/transferring-clan-ownership-mobile-001.png).
2. Go to **Members**.

<div align="left"><img style={{ width: '30%' }} src={transferringClanOwnershipMobile002} alt="transferring-clan-ownership-mobile-002" /></div>

3. Find and click on the member you want to transfer ownership to.
4. Select **Transfer Ownership**.

<div align="left"><img style={{ width: '30%' }} src={transferringClanOwnershipMobile003} alt="transferring-clan-ownership-mobile-003" /></div>

5. Check the confirmation box and click **Transfer** to complete.

<div align="left"><img style={{ width: '30%' }} src={transferringClanOwnershipMobile004} alt="transferring-clan-ownership-mobile-004" /></div>
